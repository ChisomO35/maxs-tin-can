The Holy Grail Challenge

#HolyGrailAtMaxs

Starting each Spring Semester, Max's runs the Holy Grail Challenge, a team competition built around the bar's signature drink.

The challenge brings the whole bar together and ends with a seriously good reward for the winning team.

How it works

→Each team has one captain who forms the team.
→Each captain can add up to 11 additional members.
→Holy Grails are purchased by team members throughout the semester.
→Once 100 Holy Grails are purchased collectively, the winning team earns a 2-hour weeknight private event!

Must be 21+ to participate. Speak with a bartender downstairs to join.
