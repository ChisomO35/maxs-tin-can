# Max's Tin Can — Website Breakdown

## Overview
Single-page or small multi-page site for a UNC sports-themed bar at 201 E. Franklin St, Chapel Hill. Should feel casual, spirited, and tied to UNC culture.

## Pages

- **Home / Landing** — Hero image, tagline, hours, address, quick links
- **About** — Story behind the name (Max the golden retriever + the Tin Can), owner background, UNC connection
- **Events & Specials** — Weekly drink specials, game day hours, themed events
- **Menu / Drinks** — What's on offer (cans, drafts, food if applicable)
- **Contact / Find Us** — Map embed, email, social links

## Key Components

- Navbar with logo
- Hero section (big photo + tagline)
- Hours & location card
- Events/specials list (easy to update)
- Photo gallery or image grid (UNC memorabilia, interior shots)
- Footer with contact info, socials, age disclaimer (21+)
- Mobile-responsive layout

## Tech (Keep It Simple)

- Static site — Next.js or plain HTML/CSS, unless they need a CMS
- Hosted on Vercel or Netlify
- Google Maps embed for location
- Instagram feed embed or link

## Questions for the Owner

- Do you have a logo file and brand colors, or should we come up with something?
- Do you have high-res photos of the interior, memorabilia, Max, etc.?
- Do you have a drink/food menu, or is it just cans + specials?
- How often do events/specials change? (Determines if we need a simple CMS or just hardcode it)
- Any social media accounts to link (Instagram, Twitter/X, Facebook)?
- Do you want an email signup or mailing list?
- Do you want online reservations or is it walk-in only?
- Is there a phone number to list, or just email (tincanchapelhill@gmail.com)?
- Any upcoming events you'd want featured at launch?
- Do you have any existing branding or merch designs we can pull from?
